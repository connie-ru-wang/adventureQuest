Welcome to adventureQuest!

For students

How many ways can you make it to the end? Share your story with your classmates and Tweet us your favorite story @Thinkabit_Lab! Don’t forget to ask your parents first!



For parents and teachers

Hi parents and teachers, welcome to adventure quest! This app is a fun way for students to explore career paths through a choose your own adventure story based on the RISAC model. We currently have over 10 different stories for your middle schoolers to explore, and we encourage them to take the “test” multiple times to explore their strengths and weaknesses as they change. Your student(s) can also share their results with you through a submission form at the end of the adventureQuest page.
